I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, working in the ABKGroup under the supervision of Prof. Andrew B. Kahng. My primary research focuses on automatic standard cell layout generation and design technology co-optimization (DTCO). I also explore the application of machine learning to broader EDA problems, aiming to advance intelligent automation in next-generation semiconductor design.
